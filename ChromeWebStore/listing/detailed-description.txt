Calmodoro is a calm Pomodoro timer for Chrome that helps you stay focused while building healthier desk habits throughout your workday.

FOCUS WITH A LIVE ICON TIMER
Start a focus session and close the popup — Calmodoro keeps running in the background. The remaining time appears on the extension icon badge and updates every second, so you always know where you stand without opening the popup.

STRUCTURED POMODORO CYCLES
• Focus, short break, and long break modes
• Adjustable durations and sessions-before-long-break
• Morning vs afternoon focus lengths (time-of-day slots)
• Pause, resume, and reset controls

WELLNESS BREAK TRIPTYCH
When a focus session ends, Calmodoro opens a calming break ritual with three panels:
• Hydrate — drink water
• Rest eyes — 20-20-20 rule
• Move — stretch or short walk
Original animations — no stock clipart.

INDEPENDENT MICRO-REMINDERS
Between Pomodoro cycles, optional reminders for:
• Eye rest (default every 20 minutes)
• Hydration (default every 45 minutes)
• Stretch / posture (default every 60 minutes)
Shown as a compact toast or system notification — your choice.

WORKS ON YOUR SCHEDULE
• Active days and hours (e.g. Mon–Fri 9:00–18:00)
• Lunch block to pause all timers and reminders
• Timer blocked outside active hours

FLEXIBLE BREAK LAYOUTS
Choose how breaks appear: full window, half panel (top/bottom/left/right), popup, or compact toast for micro-reminders.

TRACK YOUR HABITS
Daily stats for focus sessions completed, breaks taken, skipped, and missed — plus micro-reminder acknowledgments.

DO NOT DISTURB
Suppress break windows and notifications while the timer and badge keep running.

PRIVACY FIRST
• All data stored locally on your device (chrome.storage)
• No accounts, no analytics, no remote code
• Open source — MIT licensed

Perfect for remote workers, developers, students, and anyone who wants Pomodoro discipline with gentle wellness nudges — not noisy interruptions.
