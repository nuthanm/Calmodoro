# Promo video script outline (~75 seconds)

## Scene 1 — Hook (0:00–0:08)

**Visual:** Desktop with Chrome open. Cursor moves to Calmodoro icon.

**Voiceover / text card:**
> "Stay focused without losing track of time."

**On-screen text:** Calmodoro — Calm Pomodoro for Chrome

---

## Scene 2 — Start focus (0:08–0:18)

**Visual:** Click icon → popup opens → tap "Start Focus" → timer at 25:00 counts down.

**Voiceover:**
> "Start a Pomodoro focus session in one click."

---

## Scene 3 — Icon badge (0:18–0:32)

**Visual:** Close popup. Work in a normal tab. **Zoom or highlight toolbar** — badge shows `24:12` … `23:58` … counting down.

**Voiceover:**
> "Close the popup — the timer keeps running. Watch the countdown right on your extension icon."

---

## Scene 4 — Break triptych (0:32–0:50)

**Visual:** Session ends → break window opens with three panels: Hydrate, Blink, Stretch animations.

**Voiceover:**
> "When focus ends, take a real break. Hydrate, rest your eyes, and stretch — all in one calm ritual."

**Action:** Click "Accept Break"

---

## Scene 5 — Micro-reminder (0:50–0:58)

**Visual:** Compact toast appears: "Rest your eyes — Look 20 feet away"

**Voiceover:**
> "Independent reminders for eyes, water, and posture during your work hours."

---

## Scene 6 — Settings & stats (0:58–1:08)

**Visual:** Quick scroll through Settings (active hours) → Stats page (focus done, skipped).

**Voiceover:**
> "Set your schedule, track skips and completions, and stay accountable."

---

## Scene 7 — Close (1:08–1:15)

**Visual:** Calmodoro logo on cream background.

**On-screen text:**
> Calmodoro  
> Free · Private · No account required  
> Chrome Web Store

---

## Recording notes

- Set a **1-minute focus duration** in Settings before recording to avoid long waits.
- Hide personal bookmarks (use guest profile or clean window).
- Record at **100% browser zoom**.
- Export **1080p**, upload YouTube **Unlisted**, paste link in store dashboard.
