2. What the Extension is

Calmodoro is a browser extension that:

  • Runs Pomodoro focus and break timers in the background
  • Displays wellness break rituals and optional micro-reminders
  • Stores timer state, settings, and daily stats locally on your device
  • Shows a live countdown on the toolbar icon while sessions run
